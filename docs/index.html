Key Features

IntelGuard is a robust Discord bot designed to enhance your server's security and streamline administrative tasks. With advanced logging, IP tracking, and automated moderation features, IntelGuard ensures that your community remains safe and well-managed.

  • Security Alerts: Instantly notify admins of potential threats, such as new accounts with suspiciously short creation times or members with similar usernames, possibly indicating alt accounts.
  • Invite Monitoring: Track invite links and alert admins when an invite reaches a high usage threshold, helping prevent raids and unwanted guests.
  • Automated IP Logging: Collect IP addresses (via external logging) when members join, enabling tracking and identification of possible alt accounts or suspicious behavior.
  • Manual Alt Management: Admins can manually add or remove known alt accounts, ensuring accurate tracking of multiple accounts under the same user.
  • Reporting System: Users can report suspected alt accounts directly to admins, with automatic logging and notifications.
  • Advanced Logging: Maintain detailed logs of security events, command usage, and user reports, easily accessible to admins for review.
